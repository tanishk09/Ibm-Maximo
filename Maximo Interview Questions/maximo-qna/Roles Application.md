## Interviewer:
**What are Roles used for in Maximo?**

## Candidate:
Roles are used to assign and manage user responsibilities in a dynamic way. They are primarily used in workflows to determine who should perform actions such as approvals or task execution. Roles are also used in communication templates and escalations.

---

## Interviewer:
**Can you tell me the types of Roles in Maximo?**

## Candidate:
There are six types of roles in Maximo:

1. Custom Class  
2. Set of data related to the records  
3. Email Address  
4. Person  
5. Person Group  
6. Set of data related to the login user  

---

## Interviewer:
**Can you explain the difference between "Set of data related to the record" and "Set of data related to the login user" type role in Maximo?**

## Candidate:
- **Set of data related to the record**:  
  This role is based on a specific field in a record or on a field in a related record such as department, site, location, owner, etc.

- **Set of data related to the login user**:  
  This role is based on a specific field in the Person record and always starts from the Person object in the relationship tree, such as the userâ€™s department, manager, or supervisor.

---

## Interviewer:
**Can you explain the difference between Person and Person Group type roles in Maximo?**

## Candidate:
- **Person Role**:  
  Assigns responsibility to one specific person or user. It is used in workflows when routing to a single individual.

- **Person Group Role**:  
  Assigns responsibility to a group of persons or users. Any active member of the group can work on the workflow assignment.

---

## Interviewer:
**What is the Email Address type role in Maximo?**

## Candidate:
The Email Address type role in Maximo is used to send workflow or system notifications to a specific email address instead of assigning a task to a Maximo user. It is mainly used for communication purposes and does not create a workflow assignment.

---

## Interviewer:
**When we create an Email Address type role in Maximo, why is only the Value field editable while Object, Parameter, Email, and Broadcast fields are read-only?**

## Candidate:
In the Email Address role, only the Value field is editable because the system treats the role as a static email destination, not as a dynamic user resolution role.

---

## Interviewer:
**What is a Custom Class type role in Maximo?**

## Candidate:
A Custom Class type role in Maximo is a role that uses a custom Java class to dynamically determine which users should receive a workflow assignment or notification. It is used when standard role types cannot handle complex business logic.

---

## Interviewer:
**A workflow was assigned to one person, but that person left the company. How can this risk be avoided?**

## Candidate:
Use a Person Group role instead of a Person role to avoid dependency on a single user.
